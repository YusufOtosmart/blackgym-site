Online Stopwatch & Interval Timer

Free online stopwatch to track HIIT, Tabata, and interval workout sessions. No download required; easily record laps, rest times, and manage your workout intervals automatically.

How to Use?

Press START to begin the timer. Use LAP to record splits. In INTERVAL mode, configure your work/rest periods.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use this for HIIT and Tabata?

Yes. You can use the Interval mode to set custom work/rest periods suitable for HIIT and Tabata.

What is the ideal rest period?

Generally, 60–90 seconds for hypertrophy and 3–5 minutes for strength training. It depends on your goals.

Are my records saved?

Yes, records are stored locally in your browser (localStorage). They will remain even if you refresh the page.
